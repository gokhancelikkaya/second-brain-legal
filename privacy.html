Privacy Policy

Second Brain · Last updated 5 June 2026

Second Brain is a personal-memory app: you capture notes (text, voice, photos, with optional location) and ask about them later. This policy explains what we collect, why, who processes it, and the control you have. We keep it short and plain.

Who we are

Second Brain is operated by Gökhan Çelikkaya (the “data controller”). For any privacy question or request, contact support@secondbrainnotes.app.

What we collect

  • Your account — an email address, or an identifier from Sign in with Apple or Google. We never see your Apple/Google password.
  • What you send — the content of your notes: text, voice recordings, photos, and an optional location you choose to attach.
  • Subscription status — whether you have an active subscription (handled by Apple and RevenueCat). We do not receive your card details.
  • Basic diagnostics — anonymous app performance and error data to keep the app working. No third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

Why we use it

Only to provide the service: to store your notes, understand what they mean, and answer your questions about them. To do this, the content you send is processed by the AI providers below. We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising.

Who processes your data (sub-processors)

  • Anthropic (Claude) — generates answers and classifies notes.
  • OpenAI — creates the semantic “embeddings” that make your notes searchable, and transcribes voice notes when on-device transcription isn’t available.
  • Google Firebase — sign-in / authentication.
  • RevenueCat & Apple — manage subscriptions.
  • DigitalOcean — hosts the backend and stores your data and media.

What is sent to the AI providers, and when. When you save a note or ask a question, the content needed to answer it is sent over an encrypted connection to Anthropic and/or OpenAI: the text you wrote, and — for voice or photo notes — the audio or image itself. They process it only to understand your note and generate a response, then return it to us. These providers act as our processors under data-processing agreements that require them to protect your data with safeguards at least equivalent to ours, to use it solely to provide the service to us, and not to use it for their own purposes or to train their models for other customers. We do not sell your data.

Where your data lives & how long we keep it

Your notes are stored for as long as your account exists, isolated per user. You can delete any note — or your entire account and all its content — at any time from within the app. Deletion is permanent. A copy of your data is available on request by email.

Your rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU/EEA or UK, you can ask us to access, correct, delete, or export your data, or object to certain processing. Email support@secondbrainnotes.app and we’ll respond within 30 days. You may also complain to your local data-protection authority.

Security

Data is encrypted in transit. Each user’s data is isolated so one account cannot read another’s. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

Children

Second Brain is not directed at children under 16 and we don’t knowingly collect their data.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, we’ll update the date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the app.

This is a plain-language policy for the Second Brain app. Review it against your final data practices and local legal requirements before relying on it.
