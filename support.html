Second Brain — help & contact
Need a hand? Email support@secondbrainnotes.app and we’ll get back to you. Telling us your device and iOS version helps.

What is Second Brain?

Drop any thought — text, a voice note, or a photo — into one chat, and ask about it later in plain language. No folders, no tags.

Free vs Pro

Free includes five text notes a day. Pro unlocks unlimited notes, voice, and photos ($4.99/month or $39.99/year). Subscriptions are billed by Apple and managed in your iPhone’s Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

How do I delete my data?

Open the app, go to your profile/settings, and delete individual notes or your entire account. Deletion is permanent. Want a copy of your data first? Email us and we’ll send it.

Are my voice notes transcribed?

Voice notes stay as voice. If you want the text, long-press a voice note and choose “Show transcription.”

I forgot my password

If you signed in with Apple or Google, use that same button to sign back in. For email sign-in issues, contact us at the address above.
